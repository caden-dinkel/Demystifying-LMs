How Language Models are Used:

Now that we know how models work and learn, what can we actually do with them? Since they are fundamentally complex token predictors with a massive “world model” of sorts, they can be used for a wide array of applications. The most obvious applications is that of a chatbot, like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and many more. However, they can also be used for any application that requires complex reasoning, planning, and even interacting with other tools and systems.
